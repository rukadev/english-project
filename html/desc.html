Juan Dahlmann's Journey

This is a game for an English project. The purpose of this project is to demonstrate the role fantasy plays in the character Juan Dahlmann's life. This analysis will place emphasis on the negative toll that romanticization of the southern lifestyle has. Below you will find tips that explain the game as you navigate. You may come back to this guide at any time by clicking the "Menu" button on the map page, and your progress will be saved. If you would like to completely reset the map, you must clear the website from your history.

World Map

The world map contains the 4 different levels that are a part of the game. Completion of a level returns you to the world map. Locked levels become unlocked as you progress.

Levels

Hovering over a level will dislay info related to that level. Each level has a title, description, "Play" button, and a "Significance" button.

Gameplay

The gameplay UI contains the level title, the quote the level is based on, and the objective to complete the level, and the keybord keys necessary.

Reflection

Reflections explain how the gameplay of the level is related to the argument, as well as how the quote ties into the gameplay and the argument as a whole.
